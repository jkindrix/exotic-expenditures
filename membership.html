Member Responsibilities

What we expect from our members

Financial Contribution

Members contribute $150 monthly to our collective fund. This consistent contribution enables planning for both short and long-term adventures.

Active Participation

We expect members to participate in at least 50% of events and attend our monthly planning meetings (virtual attendance is fine).

Idea Contribution

Members are expected to suggest at least one adventure or experience idea per year to keep our collective imagination thriving.

Community Spirit

Maintain a positive, collaborative attitude and respect for all members, remembering that our strength lies in our collective approach.

How We Manage Resources

Transparent and democratic resource management

1

Collection

Monthly member contributions of $150 are automatically collected and deposited into our LLC bank account.

2

Proposal

Members submit experience proposals with estimated costs, logistics, and potential dates.

3

Voting

All proposals are voted on during monthly meetings, requiring 70% approval for major expenses and simple majority for smaller ones.

4

Planning

Approved experiences are assigned planning committees of 2-3 volunteer members who handle logistics.

5

Execution

Funds are disbursed for the experience, with all expenses tracked and shared transparently with members.

6

Review

After each experience, we conduct a brief review to document successes and areas for improvement.
