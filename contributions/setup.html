Frequently Asked Questions

How are my bank details protected?

We use Plaid to securely connect to your bank. Your login credentials are never stored on our servers. All bank account information is tokenized through Stripe's secure platform and transmitted using TLS encryption.

Can I change my contribution amount later?

Yes, you can change your contribution amount at any time through your member dashboard. Changes will take effect on the next scheduled payment date.

How do I cancel automatic contributions?

You can cancel your automatic contributions at any time by visiting the "Manage Contributions" section of your member dashboard. Please note that cancellations must be made at least 3 business days before your next scheduled payment.

Are there any fees for ACH contributions?

No, Exotic Expenditures covers all processing fees for ACH contributions. 100% of your contribution goes directly to our collective adventure fund.
