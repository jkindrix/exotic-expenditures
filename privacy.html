Introduction

Exotic Expenditures, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at exoticexpenditures.com (the "Site") or apply for membership in our collective.

We are a social fund created for a group of friends to pool resources for extraordinary experiences, not a traditional business. This Privacy Policy applies to information we collect through:

  • Our website and its features
  • Email and other communications between you and our collective
  • Membership applications and related processes

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using our Site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information for specific purposes:

Information You Provide to Us

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you contact us or apply for membership
  • Application Information: Responses to application questions, referral information, and other details you share when applying for membership
  • Communications: Content of emails, form submissions, or other messages you send us

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our Site, including pages visited, time spent, and actions taken
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device characteristics
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Information collected through cookies and other tracking technologies (see our Cookies section below)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for several purposes, including:

  • Providing Our Services: Operating and maintaining our Site, processing membership applications, and responding to inquiries
  • Communications: Contacting you regarding your inquiries, membership status, or providing information about our collective
  • Improvement: Understanding how visitors use our Site to improve user experience and our offerings
  • Security and Protection: Protecting our Site, our members, and preventing fraudulent or unauthorized activity
  • Legal Compliance: Complying with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly allow us to share your information
  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who provide services on our behalf (e.g., website hosting, email delivery)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Protection of Rights: When necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety, or those of our members or others
  • Membership Consideration: With current members as part of the application review process (for membership applicants only)

Any third parties with whom we share information are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information and to process it in accordance with our instructions.

Cookies & Tracking

Our Site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, gather information about visitors and visits, and manage access to certain features.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the functioning of our Site and services
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our Site

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies and to delete them. However, if you disable cookies, some features of our Site may not function properly.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We limit access to your information to those who need it to perform their job functions. We use industry-standard encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted online and securely store information offline.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your information
  • Restriction: Request that we restrict the processing of your information
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your information in a structured, commonly used format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information in the "Contact Us" section below. We will respond to your request within the timeframe required by applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will post the updated Privacy Policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Your continued use of our Site after changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
